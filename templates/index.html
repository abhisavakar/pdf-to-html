About Docling PDF Conversion

Docling is an AI-powered document understanding toolkit developed by IBM Research that provides high-precision conversion of PDF documents.

  • Auto-detect: Automatically determines the best processing approach
  • Text-based: Optimized for digitally created PDFs with selectable text
  • Scanned: Uses OCR for images or scanned documents

The AI-based approach preserves document structure, tables, and layout better than traditional converters.
